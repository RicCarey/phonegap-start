NHS Choices & Direct

NHS Choices is a dedicated website where you can find up-to-date and expert advice on a range of illnesses and complaints, as well as find your nearest NHS services, such as GPs, dentists, pharmacists, walk-in centres and so on. Visit www.nhs.uk

NHS Direct provides a confidential telephone service, 24 hours a day, to give advice and guidance on health issues. Calls cost a maximum 5p from a BT landline. Calls from mobiles and other networks may vary. An interpretation service is available in many languages. Call 0845 4647 or visit www.nhsdirect.nhs.uk
