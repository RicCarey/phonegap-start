Contact Us

NHS Nottingham City CCG's Patient Advice and Liaison Service (PALS) is a confidential service that provides support, information and advice about NHS services for people living in the NHS Nottingham City area.

You can telephone us on 0115 912 3320 or 0800 183 0456 any time between 9am and 5pm, Mondays to Fridays (but not bank holidays) or you can write to us at:

You can also email us at pals@nottinghamcity.nhs.uk

Should you wish to contact NHS Nottingham City CCG about this app, to leave feedback or comments, or if you experience a problem, please email contact.app@nottinghamcity.nhs.uk.
