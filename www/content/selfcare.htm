Selfcare

Self-care is the best choice to treat very minor illnesses, ailments and injuries. A range of common illnesses and complaints, such as coughs, colds, sore throats, upset stomachs and aches and pains can be treated with a well stocked medicine cabinet and plenty of rest. Always follow the instructions on the medicine label, and consult your doctor if the illness continues or becomes more severe.

A typical secure medicine cabinet at home should include:

  • Paracetamol and Aspirin (not to be given to children under 16 or those with asthma)
  • Children's paracetamol or ibuprofen liquid/syrups
  • Mild laxatives and rehydration mixture
  • Indigestion remedy
  • Travel sickness tablet
  • Tweezers and sharp scissors
  • A thermometer
  • A range of bandages, plasters, non-absorbent cotton wool, elastic bandages and dressings
